# Executive Summary: HR Analytics Platform
## Employee Engagement, Burnout & Attrition Diagnostic System

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Employee attrition costs organizations **1.5–2x annual salary** per departed employee. This platform provides a data-driven early warning system that identifies disengaged, burned-out, and flight-risk employees — enabling targeted interventions **before** voluntary departures occur.

| High Burnout Risk | ~14% of workforce |
| Low Engagement Employees | ~17% of workforce |

### Attrition Drivers (Ranked by SHAP Impact)
1. **OverTime** — Employees working overtime are 2.4x more likely to leave
2. **Monthly Income** — Below-market compensation is the #2 predictor
3. **Work-Life Balance** — Score ≤2 triples attrition probability
4. **Engagement Index** — Each 0.1 drop in score = +4.2% attrition risk
5. **Years Since Last Promotion** — Stagnation beyond 3 years = high churn

### Departmental Hotspots
- **Sales**: Highest attrition (24%), driven by overtime and travel frequency
- **Human Resources**: Lowest engagement (avg 0.41), high stagnation index
- **R&D**: Healthiest burnout profile but moderate engagement gaps

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

### Immediate Actions (0–30 Days)
1. **Overtime Audit**: Identify and cap overtime for employees with WorkLifeBalance ≤ 2
2. **Stay Interviews**: Conduct for all employees flagged as Critical Risk
3. **Compensation Review**: Benchmark salaries for below-median income employees with high performance ratings
4. **Burnout Interventions**: Enroll High Burnout employees in wellness programs

### Short-Term (1–3 Months)
1. **Career Path Conversations**: For all employees with StagnationIndex > 0.5
2. **Flexible Work Policies**: Roll out hybrid/remote arrangements in high-attrition departments
3. **Manager Training**: Equip people managers with engagement conversation frameworks
4. **Recognition Programs**: Deploy real-time recognition tools for high-engagement reinforcement

### Long-Term (3–12 Months)
1. **Predictive Monitoring**: Integrate platform into HRMS for real-time attrition scoring
2. **Promotion Cadence**: Establish 18-month maximum promotion cycle policy
3. **Culture Measurement**: Quarterly pulse surveys feeding into engagement model
4. **Internal Mobility**: Launch internal job board with career pathing tools
